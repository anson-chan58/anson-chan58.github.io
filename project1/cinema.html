Hong Kong in Cinema

Hong Kong and movie making have been closely intertwined since the latter half of the 20th century, with many films taking place in Hong Kong and or produced by and starring local talent.

Prominent actors such as Bruce Lee and Jackie Chan hail from Hong Kong, while a countless number of film locations feature Hong Kong's natural and manmade enviromnents.
