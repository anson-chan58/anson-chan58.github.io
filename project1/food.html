Food in Hong Kong

While many of Hong Kong's dishes will be familiar to those who enjoy Chinese cuisine, there are quite a few regional variations as well.

You can also find street food vendors and vegan and vegetarian options aplenty.

Local Food Glossary

Yum Cha
Literally means "drink tea," but typically refers to a specific kind of lunch course.
Dim Sum
Small portions of specific kinds of food that can be shared. Think shrimp dumplings, meatballs, etc.
